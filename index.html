üéÆ Getting Started

Welcome to Law Practice Simulator!

You play as a young criminal lawyer building your practice. Your goal is to win cases, build your reputation, and make a difference in the justice system.

Main Stats

  • Reputation: Your standing in the legal community (0-100). Higher reputation unlocks better cases.
  • Money: Earn from winning cases. Spend on office upgrades.
  • Energy: Required for investigations. Regenerates after cases.
  • Level: Increases as you win cases. Unlocks harder cases.

Core Values

  • Ethics Score: Your commitment to ethical practice
  • Justice Score: Your dedication to true justice
  • Kindness Score: Your compassion for clients

These scores unlock special psychology insights and affect your story path.

‚öñÔ∏è Case System

Case Phases

Each case has 5 phases you must complete:

  1. Client Meeting: Talk to your client. Your responses affect client satisfaction and case outcomes.
  2. Investigation: Search locations to collect evidence. Costs energy but strengthens your case.
  3. Preparation: Choose your defense strategy based on collected evidence.
  4. Trial: Present evidence, cross-examine witnesses, and make closing statements.
  5. Decision: Make the final choice that determines the case outcome.

Case Difficulty

  • Easy: Available from level 1. Good for learning the game.
  • Medium: Available from level 2. More challenging decisions.
  • Hard: Available from level 3. Complex cases with high stakes.

Making Decisions

Every decision affects your stats differently. Consider:

  • Ethical choices (green border) often boost ethics and justice scores
  • Some decisions increase reputation but may lower ethics
  • Balance short-term gains with long-term values

üí∞ Resource Management

Money

  • Earn money by winning cases (base reward + evidence bonus)
  • Spend on office upgrades to improve your practice
  • Higher difficulty cases offer better rewards
  • Monthly expenses reduce your income

Energy

  • Required for investigations and taking new cases
  • Each investigation location costs 10-20 energy
  • Regenerates after completing cases
  • Office upgrades can increase max energy and regeneration

Client Satisfaction

  • Affects case outcomes and reputation
  • Improved by office upgrades and good client interactions
  • High satisfaction leads to better referrals

üè¢ Office Management

Upgrading Your Office

Click "My Office" to view and purchase upgrades. Upgrades are organized by category:

  • Staff & Personnel: Hire assistants, paralegals, or partners
  • Facilities: Improve your office space (library, conference room, etc.)
  • Equipment: Technology and tools to improve efficiency
  • Decorations: Aesthetic improvements that boost client satisfaction
  • Services: Premium services for clients

Upgrade Benefits

  • Reduce energy costs for investigations
  • Increase reputation gain from cases
  • Boost client satisfaction
  • Increase maximum energy capacity
  • Improve evidence collection

Firm Growth

Your firm size changes as you level up and purchase upgrades:

  • Solo Practice: Starting level
  • Small Firm: Level 3+
  • Partnership: Level 7+ or with Law Partner upgrade
  • Elite Law Firm: Level 10+ or with Penthouse Office

üß† Psychology Insights

What Are Psychology Insights?

Special abilities you unlock as you progress. These power-ups give you advantages during cases.

How to Unlock

  • Body Language Reading: Win 2 cases
  • Jury Persuasion: Win 4 cases
  • Client Calming: Reach 20 kindness score
  • Ethical Clarity: Reach 30 ethics score
  • Truth Detection: Reach 40 justice score

Using Insights

Click "Use Psychology Insight" during a case to activate special abilities:

  • Gain bonus reputation
  • Reveal hidden information
  • Improve case outcomes
  • Boost your core values

üèÜ Achievements

What Are Achievements?

Milestones you unlock by reaching specific goals. There are 20 achievements to discover!

Types of Achievements

  • Progress: Win cases, reach levels, collect evidence
  • Stats: Reach high scores in reputation, ethics, justice, kindness
  • Wealth: Accumulate money
  • Mastery: Complete special challenges
  • Collection: Unlock all insights, buy all upgrades

Viewing Achievements

Click "Achievements" anytime to see your progress. Unlocked achievements are highlighted in gold!
